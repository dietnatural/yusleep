Privacy Policy for Diet Natural

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Welcome to Diet Natural. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Diet Natural collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our application and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Diet Natural, we may collect the following information:

  • Pinterest account information authorized through Pinterest OAuth
  • Profile information such as username and profile image
  • Pins, boards, and content you authorize access to
  • Usage and analytics information
  • Device and browser information
  • Log data including IP address and timestamps

We only collect data necessary to provide and improve the service.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide Pinterest-related automation features
  • Publish and manage content on behalf of users
  • Improve application functionality and user experience
  • Monitor performance and prevent abuse
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

3. Pinterest API Usage

Diet Natural uses the Pinterest API in accordance with the Pinterest Developer Guidelines. Users authenticate securely through Pinterest OAuth authorization. Diet Natural may access Pinterest account data only after explicit user permission is granted. Access tokens are stored securely and may be revoked by the user at any time through Pinterest account settings. This site and app is not endorsed by or affiliated with Pinterest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user information. We do not resell or redistribute Pinterest content or Pinterest-derived data to third parties. If a user disconnects from our app, all Pinterest-derived data is immediately deleted.

5. Analytics and Cookies

Diet Natural uses Google Analytics to improve performance and analyze traffic. Users may disable cookies through browser settings.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and restricted access controls.

7. Data Retention

We retain user information only as long as necessary to provide services. Analytics data is retained for 26 months. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

8. User Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

Diet Natural is not intended for children under the age of 13.

10. Third-Party Services

Diet Natural integrates with Pinterest, Google Analytics, and GitHub Pages. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.
