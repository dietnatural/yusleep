Fix Your Circadian Rhythm First

Get 10 minutes of morning sunlight within an hour of waking. This single habit resets your melatonin cycle and makes falling asleep 60% easier by evening — completely free.

The 4-7-8 Breathing Technique

Inhale 4 sec → hold 7 sec → exhale 8 sec. This activates your parasympathetic nervous system and lowers cortisol within minutes. Most people fall asleep before finishing 3 cycles.

03

Lower Your Core Temperature

Keep your bedroom at 65–68°F (18–20°C). Take a warm shower 90 minutes before bed — the cooling effect after signals your brain it's time to sleep. Works every time.

04

Caffeine has a 6-hour half-life. That 3pm coffee is still 50% active at 9pm, blocking adenosine — your sleep pressure hormone. Switch to herbal tea after noon.
